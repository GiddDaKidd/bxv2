About

We are a network of technical engineers in crafts such as marketing, business development, and software development working side by side for you!

Why we started and why we do what we do.

BxMark was created to ultimately help small and medium sized businesses navigate the crazy world of digital marketing. After many iterations, we found the best approach was to make it easy for SMBs to find great agencies and digital marketing professionals.

To do this BxMark created a marketplace that provides a comprehensive directory of trusted marketing service professionals in local markets and across industry verticals.
